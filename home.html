About

This project started as a way to occupy myself in the depths of winter. I keep a list of the funniest quotes and moments my friends say; at one point noticed I had a little collection related to me specifically. I had been wanting to dive back into visual design for a while at that point, and one day the idea to make movie posters based on these quotes popped into my head. To push myself, I wanted to create posters with different, distinct styles, which is why I looked for a wide variety of movie genres for inspiration.

I love this project! It's like a little time capsule of good memories. Designing the posters was challenging, but super rewarding. After making this series I feel like I'm back to loving and appreciating visual design after a long period of burnout.
